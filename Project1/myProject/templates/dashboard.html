Hello, Ayush ! Lets Get started with TTGS

Welcome to TTGS, a timetable management system for colleges and universities to schedule their organization's timetable TTGS started off as a company that formed strong relationship with schools, colleges and universities to schedule their organization to help them to generate their timetable with ease and maximum efficiency thus saving the organization's valuable time. Get started by refering the flow of the system below.

1. Add Teachers

Navigate to the "Add Teachers" section and add their corresponding details. Incorrect Details can also be removed via the "Edit Teacher" option.

2. Add Rooms

Navigate to the "Add Rooms" section and add their corresponding details. Incorrect Details can also be removed via the "Edit Room" option.

3. Add Timmings

Navigate to the "Add Timmings" section and add their corresponding details. Incorrect Details can also be removed via the "Edit Timming" option.

4. Add Courses

Navigate to the "Add Courses" section and add their corresponding details. Incorrect Details can also be removed via the "Edit Course" option.

5. Add Departments

Navigate to the "Add Departments" section and add their corresponding details. Incorrect Details can also be removed via the "Edit Department" option.

6. Add Sections

Navigate to the "Add Sections" section and add their corresponding details. Incorrect Details can also be removed via the "Edit Section" option.
